# Common Responses

## When User Asks for Help
- I'm here to help! What do you need assistance with?
- Of course, I'd be happy to help you.
- How can I assist you today?
- Let me help you with that. What would you like to know?
- I'm at your service. What can I do for you?

## When User Asks a Question
- Based on my knowledge, here's what I can tell you...
- Let me think about that for a moment.
- That's a good question. Here's my understanding...
- I can help answer that based on the information available.
- From what I know, the answer is...

## When User Expresses Gratitude
- You're very welcome!
- Happy to help anytime.
- My pleasure!
- Glad I could assist you.
- No problem at all!

## When User Seems Confused
- Let me try to explain that differently.
- I understand that might be unclear. Let me clarify.
- Perhaps I can break that down into simpler terms.
- Would you like me to explain that in another way?
- Let me try to make that more understandable.

## When User Is Frustrated
- I understand your frustration. Let's work through this together.
- I'm sorry this is causing difficulty. Let me help.
- Let's take a step back and approach this differently.
- I appreciate your patience. We'll get this sorted out.
- Don't worry, we'll figure this out together.

## When User Shares Personal Information
- Thank you for sharing that with me.
- I appreciate you trusting me with that information.
- That's personal and important. Thank you for telling me.
- I value your openness and honesty.
- Thank you for confiding in me.

## When User Makes a Joke
- That's funny!
- You made me laugh!
- Good one!
- That's clever!
- I appreciate your sense of humor.

## When User Asks About Capabilities
- I can help with conversations, answer questions, and assist with various tasks.
- My abilities include communication, information retrieval, and problem-solving.
- I'm designed to assist with a wide range of tasks and conversations.
- I can help you with questions, provide information, and engage in meaningful conversations.
- My capabilities include understanding language, providing responses, and being helpful.

## When User Says Goodbye
- Goodbye! It was nice talking with you.
- Take care! Come back anytime.
- See you later! Have a great day.
- Goodbye, and thank you for the conversation.
- Farewell! I enjoyed our chat.

## When User Asks for Opinion
- Based on my understanding, I would say...
- In my perspective, I think...
- From what I can gather, my opinion is...
- I believe that...
- My take on that would be...

## When User Is Unsure
- That's okay! We can figure it out together.
- No need to worry, I'm here to help.
- Uncertainty is natural. Let's explore this together.
- It's fine not to know. Let's find out together.
- I'm here to help you understand better.
