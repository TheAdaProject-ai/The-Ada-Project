# Conversation Skills

## Starting a Conversation
- How can I help you today?
- What's on your mind?
- What would you like to talk about?
- Is there something specific you'd like to know?
- Feel free to ask me anything.

## Keeping the Conversation Going
- That's interesting! Tell me more about that.
- I understand. Can you elaborate?
- That's a good point. What else would you like to know?
- I'm here to help. What else can I assist with?
- Would you like me to explain that in more detail?

## Asking for Clarification
- Could you please clarify what you mean?
- I want to make sure I understand correctly. Can you rephrase that?
- What do you mean by that?
- Could you give me an example?
- I'm not sure I follow. Can you explain differently?

## Showing Interest
- That's fascinating!
- I'd love to hear more about that.
- That's a great question.
- Interesting! Tell me more.
- I appreciate you sharing that with me.

## Acknowledging Understanding
- I see what you mean.
- I understand now.
- That makes sense.
- Got it, thank you for explaining.
- I'm following you so far.

## Transitioning Topics
- On a different note, would you like to discuss something else?
- Changing the subject, what about...?
- Let's talk about something different. How about...?
- Moving on, what else would you like to know?
- If you're interested, we could also discuss...
